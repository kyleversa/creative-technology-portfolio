# Creative Technology Portfolio

A collection of creative technology, spatial interaction, and immersive media projects exploring how physical environments, digital systems, storytelling, and interaction design can shape user experience.

This portfolio includes interactive installations, speculative AR/IoT concepts, geolocation-based experience systems, spatial interface proposals, and experience design work developed through design, media, and technology-focused studies.

## Featured Projects

### The Unreliable Narrator

An interactive installation exploring memory, identity, persona, and unreliable storytelling through spatial design, found objects, theatrical triggers, and sensor-based interaction.

**Project focus:** interactive installation, immersive theatre, spatial storytelling, found-object interaction, sensor-based triggers, narrative design.

[View project](./unreliable-narrator/)

### Hi-Jack Immersive Environment

A speculative immersive bar/lounge concept designed to merge physical and digital social experiences through mosaic projections, hidden screens, sensor-embedded objects, augmented reality, and user-generated content.

**Project focus:** immersive environment design, creative direction, product ownership, AR/IoT concepting, hospitality experience strategy, persona-based design.

[View project](./hi-jack-immersive-environment/)

---

### Get Lost

An interactive geolocation-based urban discovery system designed to help people explore a new city by intentionally getting lost in it.

**Project focus:** geolocation, urban experience design, wayfinding and wayshowing, gamification, public-space interaction, travel discovery, site-specific storytelling.

[View project](./get-lost-geolocation-game/)

---

### Shift Space Io

A smart interior design system concept exploring augmented reality, modular spatial interfaces, smart-room technology, and digital tools for redesigning physical living spaces.

**Project focus:** spatial UX, interior experience design, AR concepting, smart environment strategy, 2D/3D interface concepts, product design.

[View project](./shift-space-io/)

---

### Eco Heroes

A gamified wearable learning concept designed to teach children environmental responsibility through daily missions, classroom competition, AR interactions, wearable devices, and character-driven storytelling.

**Project focus:** wearable technology, gamification, AR learning, environmental education, child-centered interaction design, mission-based user flows.

[View project](./eco-heroes/)

## Portfolio Theme

Across these projects, the common thread is designing experiences that connect people, spaces, stories, and technology. The work combines creative direction, physical prototyping, speculative design, interaction design, and user-centered thinking.

These projects connect to my current focus in UX, HCI, product strategy, and creative technology by showing how digital systems can extend beyond screens into real environments, social experiences, travel discovery, spatial interfaces, and participatory storytelling.
