# Hi-Jack Immersive Environment

**Immersive Environment / Interactive Media / Experience Design / AR & IoT Concept**

Hi-Jack is a speculative immersive bar and lounge concept designed to merge physical and digital social experiences. The project explores how storytelling, spatial design, interactive media, sensor-embedded objects, augmented reality, and user-generated content can transform a hospitality environment into a continuous, participatory experience.

The concept was developed as part of an Interactive Media Management project and presented as a full experience proposal. My role included creative direction and product ownership, helping shape the overall concept, experience strategy, and interaction model.

## Project Question

> How can we create a social space that offers a continuous and fluid immersive experience that seamlessly merges the physical and digital worlds?

## Concept Overview

Hi-Jack reimagines the bar/lounge as an interactive environment built around the story of an eccentric inventor named Jack. The space combines raw, organic materials with repurposed technologies, hidden screens, projections, sensor-embedded objects, and augmented reality markers.

Rather than treating digital media as a separate layer, the concept integrates interaction directly into the physical environment. Guests are encouraged to discover, manipulate, and contribute to the space in real time.

## Core Experience Elements

The proposed environment includes:

- Mosaic projections generated through user-created content
- Hidden touch screens embedded into repurposed objects
- Sensor-embedded IoT objects that respond to guest interaction
- Augmented reality markers distributed throughout the space
- A physical-digital narrative built around the fictional inventor Jack
- A modular social environment designed for discovery, play, and collaboration

## Interaction Design

Hi-Jack uses interactive media to create a social experience that changes based on guest participation. Users can influence projections, uncover hidden narrative layers, interact with objects, and contribute to the evolving atmosphere of the bar.

The goal is to make the environment feel alive, responsive, and collaborative, turning the social space itself into part of the experience.
